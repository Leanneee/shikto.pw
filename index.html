Make a payment

Welcome to our online payment page. Our website will direct you to a secure system that allows you to pay your programme fees via Royal Bank of Scotland's WorldPay service.

To make a payment you will need your Student Reference Number (SRN), which you can find on your invoice. Please enter the name of the student as it appears on our records and then select the card type. Please note we do not accept American Express.

Once your payment has been successful, you will receive an e-mail from World Pay confirming the payment and a transaction number, which you should retain as confirmation of payment.

If you have any queries please do not hesitate to contact the student finance office at studentfinance@bpp.com or on 03300 603 100.
